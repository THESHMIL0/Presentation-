RESEARCH PRESENTATION

STUDY OF AI IN E-COMMERCE

CHAPTER 1

Introduction

Introduction

  • AI is essential in the digital era.
  • Enables learning, reasoning, and decision-making.
  • Used in healthcare, finance, and education.
  • Transformed the E-commerce sector.
  • Improves efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Introduction (Cont.)

  • E-commerce involves buying/selling over the internet.
  • Platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra changed shopping.
  • AI uses ML, NLP, and Computer Vision to analyze behavior.
  • Provides personalized shopping experiences.
  • Suggests products based on past purchases.
  • Improves service via chatbots and inventory management.
  • Identifies fraud and secures transactions.

AI in Indian E-Commerce

  • India has a fast-growing digital economy.
  • Affordable smartphones boosted online shopping.
  • Digital India initiatives support growth.
  • Companies reach urban and rural customers via AI.
  • AI manages data and 24/7 multi-language support.

Key Objectives

  • Study AI's role in personalized shopping.
  • Assess future AI opportunities in e-commerce.
  • Analyze customer satisfaction levels.
  • Identify major roles of AI.
  • Provide integration suggestions.

Scope of the Study

  • Studies customer behavior and searches.
  • Provides personalized product recommendations.
  • AI Chatbots for instant query resolution.
  • Fraud detection for secure transactions.
  • Voice and visual search technology.

Importance of Study

  • Changes how businesses operate and consumers shop.
  • Relevant to India's growing digital economy.
  • Crucial for improving business efficiency.
  • Helps businesses compete in the online market.

CHAPTER 3

Role of AI

3.1 Introduction

  • AI is used in smartphones, homes, and cars.
  • Transforming Indian E-commerce.
  • Chatbots and voice search enhance communication.

3.2 Customer Experience

  • Faster and easier online shopping.
  • 24/7 chatbot support.
  • Detects fake reviews to build trust.

3.2.2 Sales & Conversion

  • Shows right products at the right time.
  • Reduces abandoned carts via reminders.
  • Chatbots solve doubts instantly.

3.2.3 Personalization

  • Analyzes search history and interests.
  • Shows personalized recommendations.
  • Makes customers feel valued and understood.

3.2.4 Efficiency

  • Automates data entry and scheduling.
  • Reduces human errors and saves time.
  • Allows focus on creative tasks.

3.3.5 Cost Reduction

  • Reduces manual labor costs.
  • Better inventory and demand management.
  • Predicts equipment problems early.

3.3.6 Data Analytics

  • Quickly analyzes large amounts of data.
  • Identifies trends humans might miss.
  • Leads to smarter, faster decisions.

3.3.7 Customer Support

  • Instant support via virtual assistants.
  • Handles repetitive queries for order status.
  • Learns and improves over time.

Research Methodology

  • Uses Primary and Secondary Data.
  • Primary data from user questionnaires.
  • Survey among regular e-commerce users.
  • Focus on satisfaction and AI features.

CHAPTER 4

Data Analysis

Demographic Profile

  • Age: 88% are in 18-30 group.
  • Gender: 71.3% female majority.
  • Occupation: 83.3% Students.
  • Education: Mostly Under Graduates.

AI Awareness

  • 84% aware of AI in shopping.
  • 61.3% see positive impact on experience.
  • 52% neutral on specific tool familiarity.

Personalized Data

  • 60% find recommendations useful.
  • 60.7% neutral on suggestion accuracy.
  • Indicates room for AI improvement.

Key AI Benefits

  • Efficiency: 68.7% value time saving.
  • Search: 56.7% prioritize speed.
  • Support: 42.7% value chatbots.

Preferred Platforms

  • Flipkart (64%), Meesho (56%), Myntra (49%).
  • 52.7% shop occasionally.
  • 81.4% likely to recommend platform.

Satisfaction Factors

  • Product Quality (73.3%) is top priority.
  • Delivery Speed (52.7%) is critical.
  • Customer Support (40.7%) follows.

Transaction Security

  • 73% believe AI is faster at fraud detection.
  • 46% rate AI as "Effective".
  • Confidence in combined alerts/blocking.

Privacy Paradox

  • 40.4% lack trust sharing data.
  • 50% share data if service improves.
  • Trust remains a primary challenge.

Future Expectations

  • Smart Price Comparisons (54.1%).
  • Immersive Virtual Try-ons (44.5%).
  • Instant Chatbots (37%).

CHAPTER 5

Findings & Conclusion

General Findings

  • AI improves platform functionality.
  • Users are primarily tech-savvy students.
  • AI makes shopping faster and easier.

Habits Findings

  • Product quality matters most.
  • Delivery speed affects satisfaction.
  • Very few are unhappy with AI services.

Study Limitations

  • Based on 150 respondents in Goa.
  • May not represent larger populations.
  • Potential personal bias in surveys.

General Suggestions

  • Improve transparency and privacy.
  • Use secure systems and monitor AI.
  • Combine AI with human support.

Business Suggestions

  • Small businesses can use basic AI tools.
  • Focus on chatbots and recommendations.
  • Improve logistics using AI.

Conclusion

  • AI is transforming the industry.
  • Drastically improves personalization.
  • Impact is highly positive overall.
