RESEARCH PRESENTATION

STUDY OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN E-COMMERCE

CHAPTER 1: Introduction

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) is one of the most important technologies in the digital era.
  • Al enables machines to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, like learning, reasoning, and decision-making.
  • Al is being used in many sectors such as healthcare, education, finance, and entertainment.
  • However, one of the major sectors transformed by Al is E-commerce.
  • Al has become a powerful tool that improves efficiency, innovation, and customer satisfaction in online shopping.

Introduction (Continued)

  • E-commerce, or electronic commerce, refers to buying and selling goods and services over the internet.
  • Over the years, online platforms have changed traditional shopping methods.
  • With platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and Meesho, customers can shop anytime and from anywhere.
  • As the global e-commerce industry grows rapidly, businesses are looking for new ways to attract and retain customers.
  • This is where Artificial Intelligence plays a major role. Al technologies, such as machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and computer vision, are used to analyze customer data and behavior.
  • By understanding what customers want, businesses can provide a highly personalized shopping experience.
  • For example, Al can suggest products based on a customer's past purchases or searches.
  • Moreover, Al improves customer service through chatbots, helps businesses manage their inventory, and provides advanced search options like voice and image search.
  • It also helps in identifying fraud and making secure online transactions.

Al in Indian E-Commerce

  • India has one of the fastest-growing digital economies in the world.
  • With a massive increase in internet users and the availability of affordable smartphones, millions of Indians are now shopping online.
  • The government's initiatives like Digital India have also supported this digital growth.
  • In this competitive market, e-commerce companies in India are using Al to reach both urban and rural customers.
  • Al helps businesses in India manage huge amounts of customer data, improve marketing strategies, and provide 24/7 support in multiple local languages.
  • This study focuses on how Al is used in the e-commerce sector, the benefits it offers to both

CHAPTER 4: Data Analysis & Interpretation

Demographic Profile of Respondents

  • Age: Predominantly young adults; 88% are in the 18-30 age group.
  • Gender: A strong female majority at 71.3%.
  • Occupation: The sample is heavily dominated by Students (83.3%).
  • Education: Most are Under Graduates (47.3%) or in Higher Secondary (38%).
  • Insight: The data primarily reflects the opinions of a tech-savvy, younger generation.

Al Awareness & General Perception

  • High Awareness: 84% of respondents are aware that Al is used in online shopping platforms.
  • Positive Impact: 61.3% believe Al improves their overall shopping experience.
  • Familiarity Gap: While awareness is high, 52% remain Neutral about their familiarity with specific Al tools.

Data: Personalized Recommendations

  • Utility: 60% of users find personalized recommendations useful.
  • Neutrality: 38% remain neutral, indicating room for Al to become more accurate.
  • Satisfaction: 60.7% are neutral regarding the accuracy of Al suggestions.
  • Takeaway: Al recommendations are acceptable but not yet impressive for the majority.

Key Benefits of Al in E-commerce

According to respondents, Al provides value in:

  • Time Efficiency: 68.7% value Al for saving time.
  • Faster Search: Users prioritize faster product searches (56.7%).
  • Better Personalization: Tailored results are valued by 52.7%.
  • Customer Support: 42.7% see Al as a tool for improved support via chatbots.

Preferred Platforms & Shopping Habits

  • Top Platforms: Flipkart leads at 64%, followed by Meesho (56%), Myntra (49%), and Amazon (45.3%).
  • Shopping Frequency: 52.7% shop occasionally, while 28% shop monthly.
  • Loyalty: 81.4% are likely to recommend their preferred platform.

Factors Driving Customer Satisfaction

  • Priority 1 - Product Quality (73.3%): This remains the undisputed king of satisfaction.
  • Priority 2 - Delivery Speed (52.7%): Timely service is critical for a positive experience.
  • Secondary Factors: Customer Support (40.7%) and Offers/Recommendations (34%) follow in importance.

Data: Al & Transaction Security

  • Efficiency: 73% believe Al is faster than humans at detecting fraud.
  • Effectiveness: 46% rate Al as "Effective" in detecting suspicious activities.
  • Trust Drivers: Confidence is highest with a combined approach (alerts, blocking, detection).
  • Knowledge Gap: 53.3% have only an "Average" understanding of Al security.

The Data Privacy Paradox

  • Low Personal Trust: 40.4% do not feel confident sharing personal data with platforms.
  • Data for Improvement: Despite concerns, 50% are comfortable with data collection if it improves their experience.
  • Insight: Consumers will trade some data for better service, but overall trust remains low.

Future Expectations

Most Desired Features:

  • Smart Price Comparisons (54.1%)
  • Immersive Virtual Try-ons (44.5%)
  • Instant Chatbots (37%)

CHAPTER 5: Findings, Suggestions & Conclusion

General Findings

  • Most users are young (18-30), mainly students.

Experience & Habits Findings

Limitations & Suggestions

Limitations of the Study

  • Study based on only 150 respondents in Goa.
  • Results may not represent a larger population.
  • Majority respondents were young students.
  • Survey responses may include personal bias.

Suggestions for Improvement

  • Improve data privacy and transparency.
  • Use secure payment systems and monitor Al regularly.
  • Combine Al with human support for better service.
  • Upgrade Al systems and train skilled professionals.

Suggestions for Businesses & Conclusion

Suggestions for Businesses

  • Small businesses can start with basic Al tools.
  • Focus on chatbots and recommendation systems.
  • Add voice search and smarter features in future.
  • Improve logistics and delivery using Al.

Conclusion

  • Al is completely transforming the e-commerce industry.
